Instructions

The blocks on the game map control the game mechanics. Example: BEMI IS YOU means you (the player) control Bemi. WALL IS STOP means Wall blocks become solid.

This is a 2-player game. You will need to have a friend join your room, or open the game in two tabs to play!

WASD – Move up/left/down/right
R – Restart level
Z – Undo last move
